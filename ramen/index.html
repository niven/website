Ramen Restaurants in Amsterdam

Vatten Ramen

Vatten Ramen has a strong feel of "respect for tradition" which is noticeable in the menu and decor. They have great noodles with great bite. When you order a spicy ramen you get a little bowl with burnt garlic chili oil on the side which is amazingly flavorful.

  • Vegetarian
  • Vegan
  • Lunch
📌 Kerkstraat 148, 1017 GR, Amsterdam

Ramen-Ya
In the middle of busy tourist streets, in the basement. It's pretty big which is nice for groups. They have a really nice Tonkotsu Ramen. If that's your thing they also have the "Mount Fuji", a massive bowl that if you eat it all gets you a plaque on the wall.
  • Vegetarian
  • Tonkotsu
  • Lunch
📌 Oudezijds Voorburgwal 236, 1012 GK, Amsterdam
Umaimon
Their signature ramen is the Chicken Katsu ramen, and many others also include some kind of fried chicken. All bowls come with a ton of toppings and they have a number of different vegetarian options. There is a monthly Special as well.
